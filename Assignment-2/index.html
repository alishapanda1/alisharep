Do I Need to Register a Business Name?

No. A business is not needed to create a website but you should read about your options in case you decide to do this down the road. As your site starts to make money, you may want to consider doing this.

Blog or Static Website?

A blog is a kind of website where the content is arranged dynamically by date and category (by default). If you use WordPress, you are free to change the content structure/layout by simplying creating a custom menu so you have more control over what kinds of content you link to.
